Our promise

To our clients

The tools that support software development have never been better, yet software itself has never been buggier! We share your frustration with these half-baked applications that don't live up to expectations. That's why we focus on delivering functional features that will delight you with usability and reliability.

We understand that software has become extremely expensive. That's why we aim for cost-efficiency. We won't charge for unnecessary bells and whistles, or up-sell you on extra features. We'll work with you to make sure you get what you want and need, not what we want to sell you. We won't lock you in with us, either: Any and all resources we create are yours.

To our technical partners

It's our privilege to work with your resources and your codebase. We promise to treat it with the respect it deserves by handling it with surgical precision. We have no intent of grandiose re-writes or undue refactors. We'll keep our changes tested, tight, and focused because we know that every line of code we write costs the time and energy of your software developers who have to work with it.

If we need scaffold infrastructure or setup builds, we will provide automation pipelines that can re-do the work with or without us.
